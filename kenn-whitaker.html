Family

His parents are Forest Steven Jr. and Laura Francis Smith (nee). He has three siblings which includes two elder brothers Forest Whitaker, Damon Whitaker and a sister Deborah Whitaker, who are all also actors.

He is also the ex-brother-in-law of Keisha Nash Whitaker.

Early Life

Kenn Whitaker was born on 8th June, 1963 in Longview, Texas to Laura Francis (nee) Smith, a special education teacher and Forest Steven Whitaker Jr., an insurance salesman. When he was two, his family moved to Carson, California.

Kenn started his elementary schooling at Palisades Charter High School, Palisades, CA. He was neither an intelligent student nor a poor one. But he was an average student who loves acting at all. He enrolled in a college to pursue his graduation later.

He was very interested in acting and had shown enthusiasm about becoming an actor.

Acting Career

Kenn Whitaker made his on-screen debut on the TV show Strapped in 1993. He made his first appearance in the film Most Wanted in 1997 who he co-acted with Keenan Ivory Wayans and Jon Voight and where he played a minor character as a bus guard. Then he appeared in the film Life in 1999 with Eddie Murphy and Martin Lawrence where he portrayed the role of Isaac in the comedy drama.

Most Wanted (1997) is his all-time best film where his performance is really great even if his role is minor. And then appearance in Life (1999) made him more and more popularity at a broad level. Following his success in both the films, he starred in the movie The Theory of the Leisure Class in 2001. Then again in 2001, he appeared in the film Last Days.

In addition to the above-mentioned films, he also made his appearances in Tracey Takes On (1996-1999), Bulworth (1998) where he played another minor character of a Henchman and Party of Five (1994-2000).

For his role in Bulworth, he gained a positive response from his fans and also nominations for Academy Awards.

Kenn only made eight on-screen appearances in total till today but he was successful in whichever roles he did.

Kenn also doesn’t have much presence on any social media platform including Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.

Kenn Whitaker and His Brother Forest Whitaker

Kenn Whitaker’s elder brother, Forest Whitaker is very famous and is considered and world-class actor and along with being an actor Forest is also a director and a producer. His popularity also led to Kenn gaining more fame. His brother and he are often compared with each other in social media because both of them are lookalike. They look so similar to each other that they can be considered identical. Due to this reason, people often get confused between them.

Kenn went viral in 2018 on Twitter when people were concluding comparisons between Forest and him.

Even though he is talented and has his own identity, his image is often shadowed by his brother Forest’s fame. Many know him as brother of Forest Whitaker.

In conclusion, even though he is not as popular as his other siblings, he started from a zero-level place to being a successful celebrity. And his story is quite an inspiration for us all.
