Zendaya Is Bridal Chic in a Gauzy White Gown at &quot;Challengers&quot; Paris Premiere

September 03, 2024
blog

Zendaya had a busy day Saturday, with back-to-back engagements for the Paris premiere of her new sporty romance film Challengers, which means we got to enjoy several masterful tennis-themed looks. While her first couple outfits were full of tennis-ball greens, she went a subtler route with her last.

Marc Piasecki

The Euphoria star seemed to embody the Wimbledon dress code (which requires competitors to wear tennis attire that is almost entirely white, per the official website) in an ethereal white gown. While we await the day she ties the knot with beau Tom Holland in a glorious wedding dress, we can obsess over this look, which brought some serious bridal energy. The custom Louis Vuitton centerpiece featured a strapless corset bodice with a sweetheart neckline and a sweeping, slightly sheer skirt that was composed of flowy, gauzy fabric with pleats throughout and a lettuce hem.

Zendaya, with the help of stylist Law Roach, added a wide white leather belt that crisscrossed at the center for an edgy touch. To up the ante even more, she wore a gorgeous diamond necklace with a center pendant, classic diamond stud earrings, and several diamond rings, all by Bulgari. As for beauty, Zendaya glammed up with rosy makeup, complete with a bronzy smoky eye and glossy lips, and styled her new caramel-blonde hair down behind her with a side part.

The “All for Us” singer’s angelic look capped off a day of outfit changes. She started her morning in a preppy ensemble composed of a boxy minidress in a green-and-white checkered pattern from Louis Vuitton by Marc Jacobs’s Spring/Summer 2013 collection, along with classic white pointed-toe pumps, a matching hairband, and small diamond hoop earrings. Hours later, she made another statement in a shiny satin highlighter-green co-ord composed of a button-up shirt with a mandarin collar, which she wore mostly open by fastening only one button, and matching low-waisted pants. For this look, she added metallic chrome platform heels with decorative straps going up the front of her foot and a small structured purse in the same reflective green.
