Jake McLaughlin-His Journey From U.S. Army To Actor

Jake was in the military service in 2002 and served in the Army's 3rd Infantry Division and his unit was one of the first to enter Baghdad.

For his efforts, he was awarded the Army Commendation Medal, Global War on Terrorism Service Medal, Combat Infantry Badge, Army Good Conduct Medal, Global War on Terrorism Expeditionary Medal, and the Army Achievement Medal. McLaughlin was also featured in Soldier of Fortune magazine and was mentioned in David Zucchino's book titled Thunder Run in 2004.
