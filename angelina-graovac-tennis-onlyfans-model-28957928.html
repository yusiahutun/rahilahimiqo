Stunning Australian tennis star turned to OnlyFans to fund her Grand Slam dream

February 25, 2024

Angelina Graovac once had dreams of becoming a major force in tennis, but the prospect's biggest shot at fame looks more likely to materialise off the court.

The striking Australian opened an account on adult subscription site OnlyFans in late 2020, at a time when her future perhaps looked uncertain at the height of the Covid-19 pandemic. Many athletes and organisations in sport faced funding concerns during that period, and it's plausible Graovac was no different.

It was around the same time that Swiss media reported the 21-year-old had only delved into OnlyFans as a means of supporting her tennis dreams. However, there's no content visible on Graovac's subscription page as of January 2023, and it's been two years since the blonde beauty was 'seen' on the site.

While she may not have needed to post in that time, Graovac's tennis career doesn't appear to have flourished in the manner she may have wanted. The youngster lost 18 ITF matches in as many outings over the course of 2022, having managed four victories in each of the past two seasons prior to that.

The last tennis-related post to appear on Graovac's Instagram was back in December 2021 when she looked back on a 'fun' year. "I was so blessed to have met so many lifelong friends and travel so much this year," she wrote at the time. "My happiest year so far."

"Be so busy improving yourself that you have no time to criticise others," the Illawarra native wrote during a trip to London in December 2020. The progression to clay courts has proved particularly problematic in recent years, winning just two of her 20 matches on that surface since 2019.

A lack of on-court success doesn't appear to have limited Graovac too much, however, as she continue to enjoy a jet-setting lifestyle. Her 2022 uploads alone comprise posts from Tunisia, France, Croatia and Serbia.

Graovac has another OnlyFans link in Vanessa Sierra, who has her own following on the site and used to upload content featuring tennis star ex Bernard Tomic once upon a time. One of Graovac's posts from 2021 showed her on safari in Kenya alongside Sierra, who parted ways with Tomic at the end of that year.

It's unknown at this point whether Graovac will continue her quest for stardom either on-court or online. At 21 years of age, the starlet has time on her hands to pick a path in the long term, having amassed more than 53,000 Instagram followers who are clearly keen on her content.
