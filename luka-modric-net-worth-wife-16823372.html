Luka Modric net worth: How much is Ballon dOr favourite worth? Who is his wife?

February 23, 2024
blog

Real Madrid star Luca Modric, 33, is tipped to end Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi’s dominance of the Ballon d’Or.

The Croatian star has helped lead his team to Champions League victory three years running, and also led his country to the World Cup final.

Tonight we will see wether he has done enough to beat the Juventus and Barcelona stars, who have claimed the prize for 10 years.

It’s safe to say it has been a successful year for Ex-Tottenham player Modric.

Luka Modric’s net worth is an estimated $7.5million (£5.7million), according to Celebrity Net Worth.

As well as his contract with Real Madrid, he also makes money from endorsement deals.

Throughout his professional career, the Croatian has made many lucrative deals.

He is endorsed by brands such as Nike, Russell Brown and MAX TV, according to Celebrity Endorsers.

After showing promise with his hometown’s youth team, Modric was signed by Dinamo Zagreb in 2002.

In the 2005/2006 season he signed his first long-term contract with Zagreb, which was 10 years.

With his pay check he bought a flat in Zadar for his family.

In 2008 he moved to Tottenham, for a then-record transfer fee of £16.5million ($21.6million).

In August 2012 Real Madrid announced they had agreed to a deal with Tottenham.

The £30million transfer fee signed Modric to the club for five years.

Modric signed another deal to stay with the Spanish club in 2014, taking him to the end of 2018.

Then Modric signed another deal in 2016, tying him to the club until 2020.

According to Spanish outlet Marca, Modric’s annual salary is around £8million.

Luka Modric is married to Vanja Bosnic, 36.

The duo got married in May 2010 in Zagreb, Croatia, after dating for four years.

His former Tottenham and Croatia team-mate Vedran Corluka was his best man at the wedding.

They have three children together – Ivano, eight, Ema, five, and Sofia, one.

Luka and Vanja met in 2007 while she was working at Mamic sports agency, who’s client at the time was Dinamo Zagreb.

However, when Modric signed the deal with Tottenham, she moved to London with her husband.

Vanja then represented him, and was instrumental in his mega-move to Real Madrid in 2012.

Vanja prefers to keep out of the limelight, and doesn’t even have a public Instagram account.
