Camilla Luddington Is Engaged to Boyfriend Matthew Alan See Her Ring!

August 25, 2024
blog

He put a ring on it! Grey’s Anatomy star Camilla Luddington took to Instagram on Wednesday, Jan. 17 to announce that she’s engaged to her boyfriend Matthew Alan.

“Sooo this happened on New Year’s Eve. I’m not sure I even let Matt get through his proposal before I started yelling YES YES YES!! Of course it was a yes! Also… Hayden approves.. 😉 ?✨✨✨,” she captioned a series of photos of her ring. Her new bling is stunning, may we add!

Camilla and Matt welcomed their first child, a baby girl named Hayden, last April. In a previous interview, Camilla told a funny story about how Matt angered her when she was expecting.

The actress was craving cream cheese at the time, but little did Matt know, she meant a certain kind. “I asked my boyfriend to pick up some Philadelphia Cream Cheese, which is pasteurized, on his way home,” Camilla explained. “Well, he walks in with some other brand, and I had a huge meltdown.”

“I was raging because I didn’t see ‘pasteurized’ on the label. Matt had to run to the store again. It was all so ridiculous,” she added. Too funny!

Oddly enough, marriage will be the main storyline for Camilla’s character Jo Wilson on Grey’s as we enter the second half of this season. Before the show’s winter break last year, fans were left with a cliffhanger after we saw Jo unexpectedly run into her abusive ex-husband, Paul Stadler, after years of avoiding him.

The second part of the episode airs on Thursday, Jan. 18, and will really focus on spreading awareness for domestic violence. “I’ve talked a lot about how this season [we’re refocusing the show on] fun and joy and laughter, and my commitment has been to make sure there’s laughter in every episode — even the ones where the stories that we’re telling are quite painful and quite dark,” executive producer Krista Vernoff explained to TVLine. “And my hope is that women will come out of this story feeling empowered. I’m really proud of this story. I’m really proud of the work that we’re doing.” We’ll be tuning in to tomorrow night’s episode, that’s for sure.

Congrats again to Camilla and Matt — we can’t wait for the big day!
