Bradley Cooper hints he's Gigi Hadid's 'biggest supporter' as romance rumors swirl

February 01, 2024
blog

Bradley Cooper and Gigi Hadid rumors have been spreading around for a while, and it looks like they’re starting to go even more public, as the Hangover star wears Hadid’s clothing line out in public. Cute!

The rumored celebrity couple were first spotted together in October, and have been sighted several times since. Neither have publically commented on the relationship but they continue to drop subtle hints.

Bradley Cooper wears clothes from Gigi Hadid’s fashion line

Although the couple hasn’t confirmed (or denied) their rumored romance, it looks like the Hollywood star is dropping subtle hints he’s a supportive partner with his latest fashion choice.

The 48-year-old was spotted walking down the streets of New York wearing a green, blue, and black striped cashmere sweater from Gigi Hadid‘s Guest in Residence collection.

Not only that, it looks like he’s taking fashion advice from the 28-year-old, as he wore a green varsity jacket over the jumper, similar to the one she wore on November 5.

He finished the hip look off with a beige beanie, cargo pants, and some Nike sneakers.

The ‘makeover’ comes after the movie star’s fashion disaster when he was pictured with the model at the end of last month.

To fans’ surprise, the star was spotted wearing Ellen DeGeneres boxers, which would have been a freebie from one of the times he appeared on the former TV show.

Sources provide inside scoop on the ‘relationship’

Although Hadid and Cooper haven’t given us any goss, sources have, as one says Gigi ‘enjoys’ being with Bradley as she “finds him more mature.”

Previously, insiders have said they were just having fun, however, another has now told PEOPLE that the model doesn’t date ‘just to date’ and is dating Cooper as she “finds him interesting.”

“Her daughter will always come first,” the source says. “She loves that Bradley is a dad, because he gets it. She finds him more mature than guys she dated in the past.

They keep spending a lot of time together. They have dinner dates several times a week. It makes her happy,” they said.
