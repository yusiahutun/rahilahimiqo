Kylie Jenner Stuns in a Tight Coral Dress From her Brand Khy

June 03, 2024
blog

Summer is a mere couple of months away, and if like us, you’re starting to curate your perfect warm-weather wardrobe, allow Kylie Jenner to serve as fashion inspiration.

A Kardashian-Jenner spring break vacation means sexy bikinis and vibrant dresses galore, and the cosmetics and clothing mogul certainly delivered in the latter category with her latest Instagram post. Jenner shared two snaps to the social media platform that see her in a gorgeous bright coral-orange dress that screams tropical island vacation. The piece, which hails from Jenner’s clothing brand Khy, features a skintight bodycon construction and an asymmetrical shoulder construction, with one side being a short sleeve and the other two fanciful straps twisted over one another (a signature element of her new collection).

courtesy of Kylie Jenner via Instagram

The makeup mogul accented the ensemble in the most perfect way possible, wearing stunning, glowy makeup, complete with a peachy blush and a natural lip that beautifully complemented her sun-kissed skin. Jenner wore artful purple flower earrings and a dainty gold chain bracelet, while styling her dark tresses in a wavy, wet-hair look. We can practically smell the salty ocean from these pictures.

courtesy of Kylie Jenner via Instagram

The mom of two has been giving her 400 million followers several glimpses of her enviable trip. On Wednesday, she shared a series of photos of herself cooling off in clear waters while wearing a sultry metallic green string bikini, accented with a chain necklace that featured a large purple flower charm in the center, a stack of chunky silver bangles, layers of silver rings, and small silver stud earrings.

courtesy of Kylie Jenner via Instagram

The day before, Jenner looked ethereal while posing in a champagne-colored wrap dress that featured a completely open back and large silver statement earrings. She wore the angelic piece in thigh-high waters as the sun glowed a deep, fiery orange at the end of sunset.
