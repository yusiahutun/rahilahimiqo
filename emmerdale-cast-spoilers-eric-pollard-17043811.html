Emmerdale legend's raunchy past as adult star unearthed

February 18, 2024
blog

The Emmerdale cast are quite the mixed bag, with some having appeared in a variety of shows before settling down in the Dales.

But no cast member's past is more chequered than Chris Chittell, who plays Emmerdale mainstay Eric Pollard.

Few can remember the soap before Pollard joined in 1986 as a wheeler dealer turned bed-and-breakfast king, who's romanced a string of ladies, including soap favourite Val (Charlie Hardwick) and more recently Faith Dingle (Sally Dexter).

But before he got his big break on one of telly's most beloved soaps, the 69-year-old tried a different route to get into acting – starring in a series of Swedish porn films.

His list of credits include the bluntly titled Let Us Play Sex, Sex in Sweden, and Practise Makes Perfect, which is also known as Girl On Her Knees.

But Pollard's porn secret isn't something the actor is afraid to talk about – in fact, he gave an insight into his colourful acting career back in 1997.

Speaking to the Sunday Mirror, he revealed: "I did films like Let Us Play Sex and Swedish Sex Games when I was on my uppers, after running up terrible bills and seeing various ventures fail.

"One was an antiques business. Another was a Jewish-Italian restaurant in Hatton Garden, London.

"I took them to pay off some heavy-duty people and clear the bills."

But Chris' daring venture into soft porn lead the actor fearing he may never work in the UK.

"I always thought I was going to be fired, even after years in Emmerdale," he explained.

"I thought I would never work in England again because of the content of those films.

"Thank God I've been on the up ever since."

And Chris has found himself sinking his teeth into a number of meaty storylines in the soap – most recently going up against the nasty Morris to stop the Dingles losing their home.

Earlier this month on Emmerdale, Eric was horrified to discover his home was to be bulldozed to make way for a new road, and bribed the developer Morris into moving their plans.

But how will Faith react when she discovers the truth? And will Pollard's lies see him getting caught out?

Emmerdale continues weeknights at 7pm on ITV.
