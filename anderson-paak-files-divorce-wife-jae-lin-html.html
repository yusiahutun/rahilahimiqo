Musician Anderson .Paak files for divorce from wife Jae Lin after 12 years of marriage

September 29, 2024
blog

Anderson .Paak has filed for divorce from his wife of over a decade, Jae Lin.

According to TMZ, the musician — who works with Bruno Mars — filed documents on Friday asking for a dissolution of marriage.

The hitmaker (born Brandon Paak Anderson) and Jae Lin (born Jaylyn Chang) wed back in 2011 and welcomed two sons: Soul, 13, and Shine, six.

He did not list a date of separation in his filing, simply writing 'TBD' in the designated area.

However, he did express a desire for Chang and himself to share joint physical and legal custody of their sons.

The hitmaker would also like to set up a visitation schedule for their children.

Anderson .Paak and his legal team are still in the process of dividing up the former couple's assets and property.

He met Chang while they were both attending music school, where he worked as a teacher's assistant.

Before meeting Chang, Anderson .Paak had a brief first marriage with a girl he met at church during his younger years.

Although he'd been married before, the artist previously told the hosts of The Breakfast Club that his second marriage was 'the one that matters.'

The Come Down singer went on to open up about the turbulent early years of his and Chang's marriage, during which they faced homelessness after he was fired from his job at a marijuana farm.

He said that the two also had to deal with the birth of their eldest son Soul, now 13, around the same time. 

Although the two were often forced to resort to squatting or couch-surfing, Anderson .Paak's wife remained by his side the whole time.

'She's the one who came from Korea, didn't speak [any] English, she's [the one who has] the balls,' he said.
