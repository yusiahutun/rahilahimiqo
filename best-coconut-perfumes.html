The Best Coconut Perfumes Tested and Reviewed for 2024

October 06, 2024
blog

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Image courtesy of Sephora

Vacation

"Vacation" Eau de Toilette

Vacation

"Vacation" Eau de Toilette

Credit: Bluemercury

Nothing implies rest and relaxation like the name of this popular eau de toilette, which was developed by the perfumers behind the scent of the famed St. Regis Hotel. Featuring a blend of coconut water and milk, along with notes of solar musk and sea salt, we're betting you'll want to wear this summery fragrance all season long.

The 7 Virtues

Coconut Sun Eau de Parfum

The 7 Virtues

Coconut Sun Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

If you're in search of a clean perfume with a vegan formula, this coconut-, frangipani-, and vanilla-scented option from The 7 Virtues might be right up your alley. And as an added bonus, sales of the top-rated fragrance support the brand's ongoing clean water project in Ethiopia.

Nest New York

Balinese Coconut Perfume Oil

Nest New York

Balinese Coconut Perfume Oil

Credit: Nest New York

For those who prefer perfume oils or rollerballs, Nest New York's Balinese Coconut Perfume Oil is another excellent fragrance pick for the summer. Combining coconut, tropical tiare blossom, and salted musk, a little goes a long way when it comes to this beloved scent.

Tom Ford

Eau De Soleil Blanc Eau de Parfum

Tom Ford

Eau De Soleil Blanc Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bluemercury

Here's a fresh and citrus-y eau de parfum from Tom Ford that's powered by a blend of coco de mer, ylang ylang, bergamot flower, and more, for a well-rounded fragrance that's both warm and vibrant.

Heretic

Dirty Coconut Eau de Parfum

Heretic

Dirty Coconut Eau de Parfum

Credit: Credo Beauty

Another of our favorite clean fragrances, Heretic's genderless Dirty Coconut Eau de Parfum uses notes of cedar, coconut CO2, sandalwood, and clove-derived vanillin for an elevated scent.

Laura Mercier

Almond Coconut Milk Eau Gourmande Fragrance

Laura Mercier

Almond Coconut Milk Eau Gourmande Fragrance

Credit: Nordstrom

This gourmand almond, coconut milk, jasmine, and vanilla fragrance smells almost good enough to eat, and especially ideal for those who like their perfumes on the sweeter side.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Nette

Coco Fleur Eau de Parfum

Nette

Coco Fleur Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

Just like Nette's coveted candles, the brand's Coco Fleur Eau de Parfum has clean, vegan formula that's distinctive, but not overpowering. Coconut milk, orange flower, and bergamot lend to its fresh fragrance profile that's inspired by the evening reception of a wedding in Mexico City.

Kayali

Utopia Vanilla Coco 21 Eau de Parfum Intense

Kayali

Utopia Vanilla Coco 21 Eau de Parfum Intense

Credit: Selfridges

You can spray this versatile perfume just about anywhere—including your pulse points, clothing, and even your hair—for a full-body experience of its Italian lemon, coconut, ambrette, and vanilla bourbon notes.

Coqui Coqui

Coco Coco Eau de Parfum

Coqui Coqui

Coco Coco Eau de Parfum

Credit: Garmentory

Here's an island-inspired coconut perfume with an edge: Combining notes of coconut with bitter greens and essential oils, this no-fuss fragrance is a perfect match for the ease of summer.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Maison Louis Marie

No.13 Nouvelle Vague Perfume Oil

Maison Louis Marie

No.13 Nouvelle Vague Perfume Oil

Credit: Bluemercury

Maison Louis Marie's best-selling scents are a staple among fragrance enthusiasts, including their No.13 Nouvelle Vague Perfume Oil. Since it features just a hint of coconut among Tuscan fig, wildflowers, and lemon notes, it's a great option for those looking to dip their toes into fruitier scents.

Maison Margiela

Replica Beach Walk Eau de Toilette Spray

Maison Margiela

Replica Beach Walk Eau de Toilette Spray

Credit: Sephora

Maison Margiela's Replica Beach Walk is one of my favorites for the season, since its fresh, breezy scent never walks into a room before I do. Plus, the warm, genderless scent is not only easy to layer, but its blend of bergamot, heliotrope, coconut milk captures the essence of salty beach air beautifully.
