Bio:
Neelam Gill (born Neelam Johal on November 30, 1994 in Coventry, United Kingdom) is a British model. She was first scouted at 14 years old, but stayed in school until graduation, so she didn’t get her start in the industry until she was 17. Neelam’s very first runway show was for Burberry in Sept. 2013, and that spring, she became the first Indian to star in an ad for the brand. She’s since done more work for Burberry, and has also modeled for Kanye West’s Adidas line.

Best Known For:
Neelam is best known for modeling for Burberry and being romantically linked to Zayn Malik.

Personal Life:
Neelam met Zayn Malik in March 2015 when he was still engaged to Perrie Edwards. The pair met up again at the Asian Awards in April, during which the model gushed over the singer, but he was still in a relationship at that time. However, Zayn and Perrie broke up in July, and in August, Neelam caught a flight from Heathrow to Los Angeles. It was rumored that she had met up with him in the recording studio while she was in town but neither have confirmed the relationship.
