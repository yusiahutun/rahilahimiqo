Native's Girl Scouts Collab Brings the Delicious Smell of Cookies into Your Shower

It’s a new year and it’s time to refresh the bath products in your shower. You know the ones that have just a few drops left and you’ve been hitting them against the shower until you’re sure the bottle is empty. You’re in luck. Native just rolled out the cutest collab with Girl Scouts of the USA. The Native products you know and love — Deodorant, Body Wash, Body Spray, Shampoo, and Conditioner — now all come in the delicious scent of the most popular Girl Scout cookies.

Thin Mints are arguably the most popular of the seasonal cookies but I have to admit I’m partial to Samoas, or Caramel Coconut as they’re called here. Both cookies are included in the offerings, as well as Trefoils, Peanut Butter, and Lemon. Each has different associated products. To wit, there are four different Caramel Coconut deodorants — the classic Deodorant, Plastic-Free Deodorant, Sensitive Deodorant, and Deodorant & Body Spray — while the Thin Mints scent is only in Body Wash, Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner, and Volumizing 2-in-1.

Ready to grab your favorites? You can’t go wrong with any of the vegan and cruelty-free formulas free of aluminum salts, parabens, silicones, and dyes.
