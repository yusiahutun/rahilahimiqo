Congratulations are in order for Lady Charlotte Wellesley who married American-Colombian business man Alejandro Santo Domingo on Saturday in Illora, Spain. Wellesley is the daughter of the Duke of Wellington and Princess Antonia of Prussia, who is a relative of Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip.
