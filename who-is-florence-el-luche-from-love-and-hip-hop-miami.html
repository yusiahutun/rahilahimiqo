Who is Florence El Luche from Love and Hip Hop Miami? Mon 23 August 2021 19:08 Cara Houlton

With the arrival of VH1’s fourth season of Love and Hip Hop Miami, brand new cast members have hit our screens, including rapper Florence El Luche.

Also a model and entrepreneur, Florence is well known among her social media, however as a fresh face on the show, what more is there to know about this musician?

Who is Florence El Luche?

Also known as Florence Dure Jacques, she was born on 29th August as a Virgo. Currently 32, Florence will be celebrating her 33rd birthday at the end of this month.

Originally from Haiti, Florence now resides in Miami, Florida with her three children and husband, Guy Marlon Dure. Although the exact ages of Florence’s children are unknown, the pair share three sons, with their youngest being born in 2014. The pairs oldest son now has his own Instagram page where he shows off different tricks on his motorbike.

Having more recently started producing music, Florence’s first single was released in 2019. Since then she now has 5 singles available to listen to on Spotify, along with a number of music videos to view on her Youtube Channel.

Apart from pursuing her music career, Florence also opened up the biggest beauty bar in South Florida in May 2021 called Riske. The salon which is covered head to toe in pink wallpaper and sparkles offers a range of treatments often featured across Florence’s social media.

Who is Guy Marlon Dure?

Featuring alongside Florence in season 4 of Love and Hip Hop is her husband, Guy Marlon Dure.

Going into business with Florence in 2016, the pair decided to go into the restaurant industry and created their own restaurant called ‘La Fourchette Restaurant’ in the heart of Little Haiti, Miami.

Sharing a throwback photo on his Instagram, Guy can be seen wearing a military uniform, having been in the Carson Long Military Academy in his younger days.

Guy now models and, along with his son Pino, has a keen passion for motorbike stunts and racing, often posting photos of his three boys on his social media.

View Instagram Post

Florence’s Instagram explored

Often posting her luxurious lifestyle, Florence’s Instagram features many modelling shots and videos of her live performances on stage as an artist.

As well as having talent as a singer, dancer and model, in a post from Florence’s appearance at the Prestigious Haitian Music Awards in 2020, Florence can be seen accrediting her dress as made by herself, also adding fashion designer to her list of skills.

Despite previously posting many modelling photos and vacation shots, Florence’s page has recently taken a turn following the earthquake which struck her home country of Haiti in August of 2021.

Having reached out to her 1 million Instagram fans asking for donations, Florence and her husband Guy have set up a fund that has enabled them to create hundreds of food and hygiene kits to send to the Haitian people.
