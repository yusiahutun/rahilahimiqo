The youngest football fans can now join in all the Super Bowl LVIII fun thanks to the Nickelodeon Super Bowl special. Find out how to watch the Nickelodeon Super Bowl for free below.

Nickelodeon and CBS Sports announced the first-ever alternate telecast for the Super Bowl, which will air on Nickelodeon during the big game in Las Vegas. The Nickelodeon Super Bowl will feature animation and graphics alongside the traditional game-day telecast. “We are so excited to just go into this massive sports event and just be goofy and silly, and make it a fun experience for kids and Nickelodeon fans,” Bill Fagerbakke, who voices Patrick Star, told People.

    • What characters will appear in the Nickelodeon Super Bowl?

    What characters will appear in the Nickelodeon Super Bowl? Characters from the long-running cartoon SpongeBob SquarePants will join the telecast broadcasting the 2024 Super Bowl.

    SpongeBob SquarePants (voiced by Tom Kenny) and Patrick Star (voiced by Bill Fagerbakke) will call the game with CBS Sports analyst Nate Burleson and play-by-play announcer Noah Eagle.
