Who is Ben Simmons:

Ben Simmons is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on July 20, 1996 and his birthplace is Melbourne, Australia. Ben is also well known as, Australian basketball player who was selected first overall in the 2016 NBA Draft by the Philadelphia 76ers after spending one year playing college basketball at LSU. He was named the NBA Rookie of the Year in 2018. 

Ben is originated from Australia. He played with NBA guard Matthew Dellavedova on the Australian team at the 2013 FIBA Oceania Championship.
