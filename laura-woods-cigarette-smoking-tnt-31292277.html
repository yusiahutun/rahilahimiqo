Laura Woods assures confused fans she wasn't smoking cigarette at Champions League game

March 02, 2024
blog

It's not often one sees TV personalities light up on the job, much less while working pitchside in the Champions League.

So fans were stunned to see Laura Woods upload a photo of herself posing with a white stick between her two fingers and an umbrella in the opposite hand. At first glance, one could certainly be forgiven thinking the TNT host was smoking a cigarette on the sidelines while she performed her European duties.

It's more common for managers and even players to sustain smoking habits in Italy, France and Spain, but the subject is still somewhat taboo in England. Former Arsenal boss Arsene Wenger grew up in a pub and has been seen smoking on the touchline, while Lazio chief Maurizio Sarri has opened up on his 60-a-day habit in the past.
