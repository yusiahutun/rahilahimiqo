The Green Bay Packers and Dallas Cowboys are heading to the playoffs. The upcoming meeting will be an exciting matchup to tune into during Wild Card weekend, so find out how to watch the Packers vs Cowboys game live without cable below.

The Cowboys earned the No. 2 seed and the NFC East division title after a 38-10 win against the Washington Commanders. Dallas seeks to reclaim the Lombardi trophy since its last Super Win in 1996. When owner Jerry Jones was asked about head coach Mike McCarthy’s future, he responded, “We’ll see how each game goes in the playoffs,” according to Clarence Hill Jr. of the Fort-Worth Star-Telegram.

The Cowboys will be facing off against the No. 7 seed Packers, who clinched their spot after a 17-9 win over the Chicago Bears. Green Bay is led by first-year starter QB Jordan Love, who recently won back-to-back NFC Offensive Player of the Week awards.

“You want to play in the big-time games when the pressure is on and be able to go showcase what you’re made of,” Love said after the team’s win over the Bears, according to Fox Sports. “Being on the bench those three years and being behind Aaron [Rodgers] and just wanting to be out there so bad, now that I have my opportunity, just make the most of it. Take it and run with it […] It’s definitely something that I was waiting for and dreaming of and it’s definitely everything that it’s living up to be.”
