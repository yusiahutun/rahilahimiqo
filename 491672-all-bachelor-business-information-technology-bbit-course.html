All about the Bachelor Of Business Information Technology (BBIT) course

July 19, 2024
blog

Most students dream of pursuing the Bachelor Of Business Information Technology (BBIT) course. However, some do not know much about this awesome programme. An in-depth look at this course, regarding requirements, institutions offering it, and fees, would come in handy.

Businesses are rapidly embracing information technology in their day-to-day operations. Professionals with a Bachelor Of Business Information Technology are vital in the link between IT and business. Here is all about the BBIT course that you should know.

Bachelor Of Business Information Technology

What is the BBIT course all about? Business Information Technology is designed to equip students with the expertise required to develop and use computer systems. A well-round graduate can use these systems to solve business problems and make managerial decisions.

A profession in this field is called a business information technologist. He is usually equipped with sufficient skills required to make in various fields such as IT, Marketing, and Finance. He makes IT work for businesses and thus simplifies engagements in workplaces.

Some of the common areas you will encounter as you study the Bachelor of Business Information Technology course include;

  • Finance
  • Marketing
  • Operating systems
  • Web administration
  • Networks
  • Management

Requirements for a BBIT course

This program is suitable for practising IT and business professionals. It Impacts practical knowledge on the application of modern technology in all business operations. However, before pursuing this course, you must have met a set of requirements.

You require an aggregate grade of C+ and above in KCSE and a minimum grade of C in Mathematics, English and Kiswahili. Relevant qualifications approved by the recognised university would also merit.

Is BBIT marketable in Kenya?

A Bachelor of Business Information Technology is marketable in the Kenyan economy. Information Technology (IT) has become a fundamental part of all kinds of business enterprises. As a result, graduates are guaranteed ready job opportunities in the country.

Having graduated with BBIT, you can work in international firms such as IBM and Microsoft. It is not compulsory that you should work in technology firms alone. There are also numerous other opportunities in non-technological firms.

What can I do with a BBIT degree? Some of the career opportunities available for BBIT professionals include:

  • Enterprise Systems Manager
  • Business Analyst
  • Systems Trainer
  • Support Specialist
  • Information Resource Manager
  • e-commerce Developer
  • Technical consultant and analyst

Which university is best for BBIT in Kenya?

The Bachelor of Business Information Technology course is an excellent choice to help advance your career. Several universities will help you meet your professional goals if you consider taking this course.

How long does it take to study BBIT in Kenya?

The duration of the study varies depending on the institution. The duration of the degree course is four academic years or eight semesters. However, in some universities, students pursue this course for three years or nine semesters.

How much is the Bachelor Of Business Information Technology fees?

The Bachelor Of Business Information Technology fee varies depending on the institution you join. Therefore, it will be convenient to visit or call individual universities to obtain the fee structure.
