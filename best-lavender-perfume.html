Expert Pick

Chanel BOY CHANEL Eau de Parfum

“I'm absolutely in love with this scent. It makes me feel decadent, even on T-shirt and jeans days.” —Annalisa

Best Sweet Lavender Perfume

“This perfume is absolutely one of my favorites! It has a warmth to it that makes it not only intoxicating, but also comforting.” —Victoria

Best Earthy Lavender Perfume

Heretic Heretic Dirty Lavender Eau de Parfum

Best Fruity Lavender Perfume

Guerlain Mon Guerlain Eau de Parfum Spray

8

Best Fruity Lavender Perfume

Guerlain Mon Guerlain Eau de Parfum Spray

Inspired by Guerlain muse Angelina Jolie, this deeply layered perfume opens with a fruity blend of bergamot, mandarin, and pear before slipping into sweet florals.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

"This is magic in a bottle! Absolutely wonderful. Classy, sophisticated, and elegant. It reminds me of places I have never been to!" —Utkarsha

Size3.3 fl oz.
Scent NotesTop: Bergamot, Mandarin, Pear Heart: Neroli, Sambac jasmine, Lavender Base: Iris, Vanilla, Sandalwood
