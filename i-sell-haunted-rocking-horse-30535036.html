'I have to sell my haunted rocking horse that grandad used to speak to the other side'

May 13, 2024
blog

A creepy rocking horse with a haunted past is going on sale later this month – but the owner has issued an ominous warning.

The heavily-worn horse is going on sale on July 30 at Canterbury Auction Galleries, and has an estimated value of between £200 and £300.

It is thought to have belonged to moderately famous British medium Dick Godden, who used it to help summon spirits from the other side.
