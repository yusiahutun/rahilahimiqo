Inside Chris Hemsworths superhero-worthy watch collection: the Thor actor dons Rolex, Bulgari, Au

August 19, 2024
blog

But beyond his action-packed performances on the silver screen, Hemsworth is also known for his love for some of the coolest and rarest luxury timepieces. Here are five highly coveted watches the Australian actor has been spotted wearing.

Tag Heuer Carrera Automatic Chronograph – US$5,900

The elegant timepiece by Tag Heuer was launched to celebrate the brand’s 160th anniversary. The chronograph features a 42mm stainless steel case along with a grey anthracite sunray-brushed finished dial. A brown crocodile leather strap completes its tasteful look. The watch is powered by the Calibre Heuer 02 Automatic movement that offers an incredible power reserve of 80 hours. Hemsworth looks like a handsome tech billionaire wearing this timepiece for Netflix’s Spiderhead, which he stars in with Miles Teller.

Tag Heuer Autavia Jack Heuer Limited Edition – US$5,900

Tag Heuer launched this limited-edition timepiece in 2017 to celebrate the 85th birthday of Jack Heuer, the great-grandson of the brand’s founder and former Tag Heuer CEO.

The watch is a remake of an iconic chronograph Tag Heuer from 1966 that was created for racing drivers. The watch features the classic panda dial and a 42mm case that houses the Calibre Heuer 02 automatic movement. The sporty timepiece looks incredibly good on the Australian hunk’s wrist, in our opinion.

Bulgari Octo Finissimo Skeleton pink-gold (ref. 102946) – US$37,000

Standing at 190cm tall, Hemsworth is a big guy. Yet the Hollywood actor effortlessly pulled off wearing the Bulgari Octo Finissimo Skeleton watch recently, which has a comparatively petite case size. The extra-thin 18-carat sandblasted rose-gold case measures 40mm in diameter and just 5.15mm in thickness. The open-worked timepiece is powered by the calibre BVL 128SK, a hand-wound movement that offers a power reserve of 65 hours. The elegant look of the monochromatic and ultra-sleek watch is matched by its matt black alligator leather strap.

Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Selfwinding Chronograph – US$95,000

Last year, Hemsworth posted several pictures on his Instagram of an 80s-themed party where he can be seen wearing a body-hugging white vest, Adidas track pants and an Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Selfwinding Chronograph on his wrist. The watch was introduced by AP to celebrate the Royal Oak Chronograph’s 20th anniversary. The 18-carat pink-gold version features a blue “Grande Tapisserie” dial and looks absolutely stunning, especially on the wrist of the Hollywood superstar.

Rolex Day-Date 40 in platinum with ice-blue dial – US$185,000

Also known as the Rolex President, the Day-Date has been a leading model for Rolex since it debuted back in 1956. The iteration worn by Hemsworth was launched in 2015 at Baselworld. The 40mm case of the timepiece is crafted out of platinum and the bezel is encrusted with trapezoid-cut diamonds. The ice-blue dial of the timepiece beautifully complements the platinum case. Rounding out the uber-luxe timepiece is the presidential bracelet. The watch is powered by the calibre 3255.
