Dancing on Ice's Miles Nazaire forced into meeting with ITV bosses after refusing demand

March 21, 2024
blog

Miles Nazaire was called into a meeting after he "made a big fuss".

The Made in Chelsea star, 28, has made it to the grand final of Dancing on Ice with pro skater Vanessa Bauer. Miles will compete against Adele Roberts, Ryan Thomas and Greg Rutherford to become champion of the series.

Reflecting on his time on the show ahead of the series, the TV personality admitted it wasn't always smooth sailing. In an exclusive chat with Daily Star and other press, Adele confessed that she's loved wearing wigs as part of her routines, but the same couldn't be said for Miles.

"I hated them," he said. "I actually made a big fuss and all the executives came in the room and told me I had to wear the wig."

Elaborating, Miles explained: "I had to wear a wig once and it cut off all the circulation in my head. When we did the Sunday run, it's like I wasn't there because it was so painful."

The TV star was forced to "put his foot down," adding: "Sassy pants came out and I said I'm not doing it with the wig. So they gave me hair extensions instead."

It's likely Miles was referring to his performance in week five, when they skated to Cum On Feel The Noise from Rock of Ages. The reality star performed shirtless, wearing a pair of leather trousers and a red bandana which sat above his brown hair extensions.

While only two out of the four finalists will perform the iconic Bolero on Sunday night, all four celebrities have rehearsed the routine. Miles admitted he wasn't feeling nervous about performing the dance in front of Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean.

"Weirdly no I'm not nervous at all," Miles said. "I'm really honoured. Vanessa and did the routine to a different kind of music and yesterday it was a really beautiful moment between us.

He added: "Being able to showcase our own version for the Bolero for them is going to beautiful." Miles went on to say that he feels less pressure because the performance won't be marked by the judges.

Dancing on Ice Final airs Sunday, March 10
