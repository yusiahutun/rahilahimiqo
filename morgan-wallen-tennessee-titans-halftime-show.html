Morgan Wallen to Play Tennessee Titans Halftime Show

April 05, 2024
blog

Morgan Wallen will provide the halftime entertainment during Sunday’s (Sept. 16) Tennessee Titans game in Nashville. The “Whiskey Glasses” singer is set to take the jumbotron stage on the north side of the team's stadium.

The stage is just above Nissan Stadium’s northern concourse, not on the field. Wallen will play songs like his No. 1 hit “Up Down” and his current single “Whiskey Glasses.” The NFL’s Titans kick off against the Houston Texans at 12PM CT.

Large venues should present no problem for the relative newcomer. He’s been readying to tour with Jake Owen this fall after a successful summer run playing stadium venues as part of Luke Bryan’s What Makes You Country XL Tour. Both “Up Down” and “Whiskey Glasses” are part of Wallen’s debut album If I Know Me, which features a mix of country themes over progressive arrangements crafted with the help of Joey Moi.

Wallen first gained national attention on NBC’s The Voice in 2014, and gained more recognition for his debut single, “The Way I Talk,” in 2016. In addition to touring with Bryan and Owen, he’s toured and written with Florida Georgia Line. In fact, he helped write Jason Aldean’s latest No. 1 hit, “You Make It Easy,” with Tyler Hubbard and Brian Kelley, as well as Jordan Schmidt.
