10 best opening prayers for school assembly before classes

July 16, 2024
blog

Every school conducts assemblies in the morning. The assemblies bring students and teachers together to do various activities such as communicating information, performing attendance checks, and conducting special programs. Opening prayers for school assembly are a sure way of starting the day with God's guidance.

Prayers are one of the best ways a school can use to pass morals and values to students. When they get used to closing or opening prayers for school assembly, students register in their minds that even without the teachers around, they cannot miss doing the right thing.

Opening prayers for school assembly

For every school, starting a day with prayers is the best tradition. The prayers allow every school member to start the day positively. Moreover, the words of wisdom or motivational passages read during the assembly play a great role in promoting the spiritual well-being of every individual.
