Emily Ratajkowski's perfect autumnal look

July 27, 2024
blog

A change in season is the perfect time for a wardrobe refresh, and Emily Ratajkowski is clearly enjoying updating her look for the colder weather this year. The model was spotted out in New York City wearing a classic autumn ensemble: a black mini-skirt, over-the-knee boots and a black turtleneck.

Her look shows how easy it is to stay stylish by keeping it simple, and relying on fashion staples that you'll most likely already have in your own wardrobe. A new season doesn't have to mean hurriedly shopping for new purchases; after all, the most sustainable fashion item is the one you already own.

Splash News

Here's why we love her look and how to recreate it yourself:

The look

Ratajkowski knows the power of pared-back dressing. She kept her accessories to a minimum, simply adding a leather belt, a mini cross-body bag and gold jewellery. She added a touch of colour with a red lip, the ultimate signifier of chic.

Over-the-knee boot
Black denim skirt
Gold hoop earrings
Black turtleneck

Why it works

Although her outfit formula is simple, it's effective. The gold hoops and red lip help to elevate the ensemble – as does the heeled boot – but the head-to-toe black keeps it looking considered, grown-up and feeling daytime appropriate.

Why it's a forever classic

The beauty of this look is that you'll most likely have these pieces in your wardrobe already, so you can recreate it for yourself very easily. Thanks to the timeless head-to-toe black and trend-free aesthetic, it's an ensemble you can wear for years to come without it looking or feeling dated. We've all most likely got a black turtleneck and mini-skirt; if you're not feeling quite brave enough for the over-the-knee boot style, this can be easily swapped for a knee-high version, or a flat over a heel.

Where to shop it

If you're feeling inspired by Ratajkowski to opt for thigh-high boots this season, then look no further than the below styles. Steer clear from trend-led colours or patterns, instead opting for a wearable pair that you'll look forward to digging out whenever the temperatures drop. Whether you believe the higher the better when it comes to heels, or you're more likely to be found wearing flats, there's the perfect style ready to become central to your seasonal wardrobe.

Stuart Weitzman
Bottega Veneta

How to style it

If you're feeling convinced by Ratajkowski's perfect autumnal look, there are plenty of ways you can make each part of her ensemble work harder in your wardrobe. Take the over-the-knee boot; it looks just as chic when paired with a longer hemline or jumper dress as it does with a mini-skirt.

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

Ratajkowski's black skirt would also look great paired with a black blazer (another item you'll likely have already) and a pair of loafers for a more preppy look.

Gotham//Getty Images

Finally, her turtleneck is a must-have for autumn, winter and beyond. It will look just as stylish layered underneath your favourite winter coat, or paired with a longer pleated skirt for the office.

Gotham//Getty Images
