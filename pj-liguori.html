Early Life and Childhood

PJ Liguori came to this world on December 11, 1990. He was born and raised in Peterborough, Cambridgeshire, England. Currently, his age is 33 years old and his birth sign is Sagittarius. He was born to Italian parents. PJ has a sister but her name is not revealed.

Professional Life

PJ is best known for his YouTube channel KickThePj. He specializes in many different artistic areas including screenplay, directing, songwriting, creating props, acting, producing, editing, sound, art, crafts, writing, and costume. His most popular video is “VOICES WITH JACKSEPTICEYE“.

Moreover, his most notable work is the comedy fantasy web series Oscar’s Hotel for Fantastical Creatures, ‘Colour Bandits’ and ‘The Forever Train’.

PJ uploaded his first video in early 2007 at age 16. He released an album titled ‘Stories from Somewhere’ on 7 October 2011. Therefore, the album is a collection of short stories told in song.

Moreover, he won a Virgin Media Award for his short film ‘PJ, Tiny Planet Explorer’ in November 2012.

Likewise, he has also been awarded for Best Director for his film series ‘Oscar’s Hotel for Fantastical Creatures’ in the 2016 Streamy Awards. He also was nominated for the British Online Creator Awards (BONCAs) as the British Creator of the Year in October 2016.
