About

Jamaican Musician Bob Marley was born Robert Nesta Marley on 6th February, 1945 in Nine Mile, Saint Ann Parish, Colony of Jamaica and passed away on 11th May 1981 Cedars of Lebanon Hospital, Miami, Florida, USA aged 36. He is most remembered for such songs as “Get Up, Stand Up,” “One Love,” “No Woman, No Cry,” “Could You Be Loved,” “Buffalo Soldier,” “Jammin’” and “Redemption Song”. His zodiac sign is Aquarius.
