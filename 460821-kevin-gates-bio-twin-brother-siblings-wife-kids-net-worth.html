His ethnicity is a mix of Moroccan (father’s side) and Puerto Rican (mother’s side) ancestry. He has a younger brother ( Brandon Gilyard) and sister.

The rapper was arrested for the first time in 1999 when he was 13. His offence was joyriding in a stolen car as a passenger. That experience had a profound effect on his life because it steered him in the direction of more crime. He attended the Baton Rouge Community College briefly when he was 17.

Career beginnings and incarceration

After leaving Baton Rouge Community College, Gates set his eyes on becoming a professional rapper. He began his career in 2005 after being signed by Dead Game Records. By 2008, he had released two mixtapes called Pick of Da Litter and All or Nuthin.

His early success was shared with other Baton Rouge natives Boosie Badazz and Webbie. The three had collaborated on his first mixtape. Gates and Boosie were incarcerated in separate cases in 2008; hence their music career was put on hold. Gates served a sentence of 31 months but was released early for good behaviour.

Return to music

In 2012, he released another mixtape by the name Make ‘Em Believe, which caught the attention of Lil Wayne’s Young Money Entertainment. Shortly after, he would partner with his wife to start a record label called Bread Winners’ Association.

His career continued to blossom, with more of his mixtapes receiving critical praise. The Luca Brasi Story came out in 2013 and helped Gates get a contract with Atlantic Records. In July of the same year, Gates’ first major label mixtape, Stranger Than Fiction, was released. It peaked at number 37 on the Billboard 200.

In 2014 Gates became a member of XXL’s Freshman Class. From 15th July to 30th August 2014, he was on tour with Chevy Woods. At about the same time, he announced the creation of a new energy drink called I Don’t Get Tired, which is a name he borrowed from one of his singles.

Wife and children

Kevin Gates' wife goes by the name Dreka Haynes, and the two have been long-term sweethearts. They officially became husband and wife on 17th October 2015 when they exchanged their vows. Prior to that, they had been dating for over a decade.
