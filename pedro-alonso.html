  • 1995: Pedro Alonso began his acting career in the theatre with the play “The Obituary.”

  • 2004: Alonso gained recognition for his role in the Spanish TV series “Padre Coraje.”

  • 2017: Pedro Alonso achieved international fame for his role as Berlin in the Spanish TV series “Money Heist.”
