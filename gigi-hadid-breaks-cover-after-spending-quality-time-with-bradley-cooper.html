Gigi Hadid breaks cover after spending 'quality time' with Bradley Cooper

June 27, 2024
blog

Gigi Hadid has been spotted in New York for the first time after ‘spending quality time’ with Bradley Cooper.

The ‘could be’ celebrity couple have been rumored to be getting close as of late, and have been reported to have been taking weekend trips together, although, it seems like they want to keep things on the down low, for now.

Gigi Hadid ‘breaks cover’ in luminous outfit

Although it seems like the model and the Hollywood star want to be keeping things low key, the same can’t be said for the 28 year olds outfit she was recently spotted in.

Walking down the streets of New York, the fashion icon was spotted wearing a bright yellow jumpsuit, paired with brown uggs and a wooly bobble hat.

The mom of one topped the look off with a pair of sunglasses, turning the cozy look chic. If anyone could pull this one off, it was going to be Gigi Hadid.

Her appearance in New York comes after her and Bradley Cooper were spotted twice together in New York.

Although, sources tell PEOPLE that the two are “having fun together,” so we’re not sure if anything serious is on the horizon just yet.

“[They have] things in common so it’s possible to see it progress. It appears super casual now, but they both have kids, big careers, busy lives and understand what life is like in these circles. It’s cute…and there is an attraction,” said another insider.

The pair are both parents to daughters, Gigi is mom to three year old Khai, who she shares with Zayn Malik, whereas Bradley is dad to six-year-old daughter Lea with ex-partner Irina Shayk.

Taylor Swift loans the ‘couple’ her Rhode Island apartment

After lending her New York apartment to Sophie Turner after her divorce from Joe Jonas, it’s been reported that Taylor Swift has once again been a great friend, reportedly loaning her Rhode Island apartment to Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper as a “secret love nest.”

“Even though Gigi and Bradley have their own properties in New York, they wanted somewhere more private to spend time getting to know each other and Taylor was only too happy to help. She said the door to her home is always open for them to use,” a souce told The Mirror.

The home is said to be where the Love Story singer hosts her iconic Fourth of July parties, and includes eight bedrooms, and of course, a pool in the backyard.
