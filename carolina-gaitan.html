Carolina Gaitan is a popular and talented Columbian Actor and Singer. She is being managed by MM Management & Production Inc. Some of her works are Celia, Isa TK+, Narcos, Sin senos si hay paraiso and many more. In 2017 she was the recipient of Premios Tu Mundo Award for her role in Celia and Sin senos si hay paraiso.

Early Life

Carolina Gaitan was born on 4th April 1984 in Villavicencio, Meta, Colombia. The information about her parents has been kept private and away from the limelight. She has a brother whose name is German Gaitan Lozano. Carolina has completed her high school education but the details about the same have been kept private. For her higher and further studies, she went to Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute located in New York. She has received professional training in the acting field.

Carolina Gaitan Career

Carolina began her career by working in various televisions roles. In 2002 she was seen as an Escarcha member in Popstars: Colombia. After three years she was seen in Vuelo 1503 as Yuli Salcedo. In 2007 she was seen in Asi es la vida as Sussana and Zona rosa as Sara Bautista. In 2008 she worked in Mujeres Asesinas as Valentina. Next year she worked as a lead role in Gabriela, giros del destino as Gabriela Rueda and Isa TK+ as Catalina Bernabeu. In 2010 she was seen in La Diosa Coronada as Valeria. In 2011 she worked in a recurring role of Alicia in Flor Salvaje.
