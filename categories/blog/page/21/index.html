Can a bottle with a clever straw banish snoring?

A drinking bottle with a clever nozzle and straw could help banish heavy snoring — by strengthening the muscles in the mouth. To drink from it you have to use your tongue to press the nozzle against the roof of the mouth; this then opens a valve in the straw that releases water from the bottle. The theory is that this action helps tone muscles at the back of the mouth that control the tongue — and this in turn means the tongue is less likely to relax so much during sleep that it partially blocks the airway, a common cause of snoring.
