It is hard to forget about Karen Fuller in the KCTV5 staff changes considering the noise she made upon her exit with her age discrimination lawsuit. She had worked for the network for 12 years (2003 - 2015) and was unceremoniously let go.

According to her, leaving the news network was not her decision, and she was not allowed to say goodbye to her audience.

In 2018, a federal judge ruled that her gender and discrimination case against KCTV5 could go forward. The news anchor had provided enough evidence to prove that she had been fired because of her age or gender. The station had created an age ceiling for its female anchors but not for its male presenters.
