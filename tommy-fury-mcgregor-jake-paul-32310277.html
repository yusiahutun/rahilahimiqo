Tommy Fury has called out Conor McGregor after being inspired by Jake Paul’s monumental upcoming showdown with Mike Tyson.

YouTuber-turned-boxer Paul announced this week that he will be fighting legendary boxer Tyson, 57, in front of 80,000 fans on July 20. The bout involving Paul, who is Fury’s former rival and lost to the Mancunian in February 2023, is expected to be one of the most lucrative boxing matches in history.

Now the 24-year-old Fury has taken a page out of Paul’s book by challenging another combat sports legend in McGregor. The Love Island 2019 winner threw down the gauntlet to The Notorious, who has only traded the UFC for one boxing match against Floyd Mayweather, which he lost in 2017.
