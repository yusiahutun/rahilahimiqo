A Look Back Into Little Richard Car Accident

Little Richard, the well-known singer from the early era of rock ‘n’ roll, was reportedly in stable condition at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center following a car accident in West Hollywood.

A spokesperson from the hospital conveyed that the singer, Richard Penniman, sustained injuries due to the accident that occurred at 12:05 a.m., as reported by Ron Wise.

According to Deputy George Moak, witnesses informed sheriff’s deputies that Penniman was driving his 1984 Nissan 300ZX at around 60 miles per hour.

According to the police investigation, the accident happened on Santa Monica Boulevard when he collided with a telephone pole at Curson Avenue.

Also, the posted speed limit in the area is 30 miles per hour. Thankfully, despite the accident’s severity, Richard was awake, conscious, and alert, according to Wise.

Moreover, there is no evidence that Penniman was under the influence of any drugs or alcohol, Moak said.

The singer was trapped in the car for 30 minutes while county firefighters used the “jaws of life,” a metal-cutting device, to pry him loose. Penniman was alone in the car.

Further details about Little Richard’s car accident are not made public as of now. 

Little Richard injury 

Little Richard suffered severe injuries in a car accident in 1985, including a broken right leg, ribs, and head and facial injuries.

Additionally, his recovery took several months, preventing him from attending his induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1986, where he provided a recorded message instead.

Moreover, in 2007, Richard started facing problems while walking due to a medical condition, sciatica in his left leg, mandating him to use crutches.

In 2009, he had hip replacement surgery but continued to have issues with his hip, often needing to perform while seated in a wheelchair.

In 2013, Richard suffered a heart attack at home, but taking aspirin and having his son turn on the air conditioner, according to his doctor, saved his life.

Richard credited his survival to divine intervention, stating “Jesus had something for me. He brought me through.”

Little Richard health 2023 

After a two-month illness, Little Richard passed away on May 9, 2020, at 87, at his home in Tullahoma, Tennessee.

Also, the cause was related to bone cancer. His brother, sister, and son were by his side when he died.

Richard’s death prompted tributes and expressions of influence from many popular musicians he had inspired over his career.

He collaborated with legendary musicians like Bob Dylan, Paul McCartney, Mick Jagger, and Lenny Kravitz and figures in other creative fields like film director John Waters.

Furthermore, Little Richard was 87 years old. He was laid to rest at Oakwood University Memorial Gardens Cemetery in Huntsville, Alabama.
