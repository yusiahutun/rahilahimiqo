Patti LuPone Rips Madonna's Acting: She's a 'Movie Killer'

April 06, 2024
blog

Patti LuPone didn't hold anything back when she critiqued Madonna on Tuesday night's episode of “Watch What Happens Live.”

The two-time Grammy and Tony Award winner made it clear that she is not a fan of Madonna's acting skills after a caller asked if she and the singer had ever met and talked about their “two iconic performances as Evita.”

“I thought it was a piece of sh*t,” LuPone said to host Andy Cohen. “Madonna is a movie killer. She's dead behind the eyes. She couldn't act her way out of a paper bag."

Both women who are icons in their respected fields won an award for their performance, but LuPone is still unsatisfied with the “Material Girl's” work.

"She should not be on film or on stage. She's a wonderful performer for what she does, but she is not an actress.”

And LuPone did not stop at just Madonna. The American Theater Hall of Fame inductee also went after “Real Housewives of New York City's” LuAnn D'Agostino and “Dance Mom's” Abby Lee Miller.

“She can't act,” LuPone said after watching a throwback clip of D'Agostino's 2011 cameo in “Law & Order: SVU.”
