Equity Bank Kenya online banking: Registration &amp; user guide

February 02, 2024
blog

We live in a world where things have become pretty easy. One of the best boosts to easy living in this century has been the arrival of online banking. With this technology, you no longer have to chase down branch managers to confirm your account balance. Through the Equity online banking system, customers access multiple services from the comfort of their homes or offices.

The Equity online banking system has revolutionised service provision for customers. They no longer have to queue in banking halls to check balances, pay bills, transfer funds, or download statements. Instead, customers can use the Equity Bank website to access various services.

All about Equity Bank Kenya online banking

Equity Online is an internet banking platform allowing individuals and business people to perform banking and lifestyle activities at home or office. These online services are available via EazzyNet or mobile banking.

EazzyNet online banking

Eazzy online banking is an innovative service with unmatched services. It is an upgraded banking platform specially developed to address varied banking needs. Follow the steps below to register.

  • On an internet-enabled device, visit the Equity Eazzy banking portal.
  • Select your country, then click Enter.
  • Click Register Now.
  • You will be redirected to another page. Click Register again.
  • Enter your country, account number, and ID number. Accept the terms and conditions, then click Register.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the process.

Precautions to take

As you apply for online banking services, observe the following precautions.

  • Ensure there is no one physically eavesdropping on you while you enter your registration or login details (unless they are trusted people).
  • Always use strong passwords to secure your account.
  • Never use a public computer unless you have to.
  • Always log out from your account once you are done.

EazzyNet services

Once you register on this platform, you will access the following services.

  • Opening a bank account instantly.
  • Managing personal finances, e.g. viewing and downloading statements, buying airtime, sending money to other accounts, sending money to mobile wallets, etc.
  • Paying bills.
  • Managing card services, e.g. stopping cards and paying credit card bills.
  • Accessing loan services.

Equity mobile banking

If you do not wish to use EazzyNet, you can also access online banking services through the Equity mobile app. The app is available on your app or play store for free, and various Equity self-service options are available. Follow the steps below to use the app.

  • Download the Equity Mobile Application from your Play or App store.
  • Open the app and click Register.
  • If you are an active customer, log in with your Eazzybanking App credentials. You will be required to update your password details. Follow the prompts to complete this step.
  • If you are an inactive or dormant customer, click Already an Equity Bank Member. Fill in the details and complete the registration process as prompted.
  • If you are a new customer, click Register. Next, create your profile and open an account. Follow the prompts to complete this process.

Equity mobile app services

Once you download, install, and open the Equity online banking app, you will access the following services.

  • Filtering statements to narrow down to a specific transaction using the date, amount, or transaction type.
  • Paying bills.
  • Managing personal finances, e.g. viewing and downloading statements, buying airtime, sending money to other accounts, sending money to mobile wallets, etc.
  • Downloading transaction receipts.
  • Topping up a card directly from your bank account.
  • Managing card services, e.g. stopping cards and paying credit card bills.
  • Transferring funds.
  • Accessing loan services.

How can I access my Equity account online?

You can access your account using the EazzyNet portal or the Equity mobile banking application on your smartphone. You must complete the registration process to access the account if you are not registered.

How do I register for Equity Bank online banking?

You can register by visiting the EazzyNet portal on an internet-enabled device, preferably a personal rather than a public device. Click Register and follow the prompts. Alternatively, you can download and install the mobile application.

You can access your account via the Equity mobile application available on your Play or App store. Once you install it, click Register and follow the prompts to access your account.
