Bel Air mansion with 18 bedrooms, 20 bathrooms for sale for $50m

June 03, 2024
blog

A Los Angeles mansion complete with 18 bedrooms and 20 bathrooms has hit the market for $50million.  

The sprawling home would be any entertainer's dream, boasting a swimming pool, games room, indoor sports court, numerous lounges, a bar, and a bowling alley.

The living space measures some 36,000 square feet and the home itself sits on 1.1 acres of surrounding land.

A sprawling Mediterranean-style mansion built in Bel Air in 1927 has gone on the market with a price tag of $50million

The property is being pitched as an entertainer's dream, with a heated indoor pool and spa area for guests to enjoy

The pool is surrounded by seating areas on three sides, with a hot tub located at the back for those wishing to take a soak

Elsewhere is a full movie theater, complete with seating for a dozen people and its very own curtained screen

The property was last sold in 2008 to the wealthy Iranian Mossanen family, who also own luxury properties in London

There are at least two fireplaces inside the home, one of which is located in this large living area complete with a bar

Another fireplace is located in this wood-paneled office, which was recently redesigned by British designer Gavin Brodin 

Built in 1927, the Bel-Air home also has two swimming pools - one indoor and one outdoor - and a movie theater.

On the lower level, around 20,000-square-foot of space is dedicated to entertaining, including an indoor resort-style pool and spa, sauna, massage room and steam bath.

The home was last purchased in 2008 by the Mossanens, a wealthy Iranian family who also own property in London, according to the Wall Street Journal.

Other reports suggested the property was being leased for $75,000 per month and was used to house the 11 finalists on American Idol's eighth season.
