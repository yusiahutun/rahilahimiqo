How to sambaza airtel credit

What is sambaza? It refers to the service provided by mobile networks for people to share their mobile airtime with others. This service is provided by Airtel also here in Kenya, where a consumer can transfer 10 to 10,000 Kenyan Shillings. The process can be executed through an SMS from individual mobile phones. Airtel took this initiative for their customers in order to be connected with others 24/7.
