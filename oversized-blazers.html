12 oversized blazers to look effortlessly cool in this spring

August 14, 2024
blog

An oversized blazer is the perfect transitional piece

    Mixing the sophistication of tailoring and the effortless style of a slouchy silhouette, an oversized blazer is a true wardrobe staple. 

    One of my favourite transitional pieces, once spring rolls around I can't wait to switch out my longline coats for something a little more lightweight, and the high street is full of must-see new oversized blazers to shop.

    From the classic blazer, jeans and white T-shirt ensemble to elevating your athleisure, or wearing one to sharpen up an otherwise very feminine midi skirt, they can be worn over just about anything. 

    Whether you're looking for a chic black oversized blazer, a spring-ready white oversized blazer, royal-approved neutrals or hot pink hues, I've found something to suit every outfit.

    Royals and celebrities wearing oversized blazers

    A capsule wardrobe essential, oversized blazers have been seen on everyone from Princess Kate to Emily Ratajkowski. Proving it's a royal-worthy look, Kate often wears one over a pair of wide-leg tailored trousers for public engagements, while I've spotted Emily nailing casual-cool in several pieces, from black leather to houndstooth, styled with straight-leg jeans and heeled boots. 

    Kate Hudson made a case for a pinstripe piece worn with jeans and loafers, and Rosie Huntington-Whiteley gave me all of the outfit goals, adding an androgynous edge to her silk midi skirt with her camel blazer. Just add a pair of heels and oversized sunglasses for a very chic spring outfit.
