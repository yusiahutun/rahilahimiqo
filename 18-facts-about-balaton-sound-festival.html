Balaton Sound is one of Europe’s largest electronic music festivals.

The Balaton Sound Festival is renowned for its impressive lineup of electronic music artists and DJs who attract thousands of music lovers from around the world.

The festival is held annually in Zamárdi, Hungary, on the shores of Lake Balaton.

Lake Balaton provides a picturesque backdrop for the Balaton Sound Festival, creating a unique and unforgettable experience.

Balaton Sound Festival was first organized in 2007.

Since its inception, the festival has grown in popularity and has become a highlight of the European summer festival scene.

Over 200,000 attendees visit Balaton Sound Festival each year.

The festival attracts a massive crowd of music enthusiasts who come together to enjoy the vibrant atmosphere and incredible performances.

Balaton Sound Festival lasts for four days.

This extended duration allows festival-goers to fully immerse themselves in the music, creating lasting memories.

The festival features a diverse range of electronic music genres.

From house and techno to EDM and trance, Balaton Sound offers something for everyone’s musical taste.

The festival hosts both established and up-and-coming artists.

Attendees have the opportunity to witness performances by world-renowned DJs as well as discover new talent.

Balaton Sound Festival boasts impressive stages and production design.

The festival offers various accommodations options.

FAQs

1. When and where does Balaton Sound Festival take place?
The Balaton Sound Festival usually takes place in early July on the shores of Lake Balaton, in Zamárdi, Hungary.

2. How do I get to the festival site?
You can reach the festival site by car, train, or bus. There are also shuttle services available from nearby cities to make transportation convenient for festival-goers.

3. Are there any age restrictions for attending Balaton Sound Festival?
Yes, the festival is strictly for attendees aged 18 and above. Valid identification may be required for entry.

4. Can I bring my own food and drinks to the festival?
Outside food and drinks are not allowed into the festival grounds. There are various food and beverage vendors available inside the festival area to cater to your needs.

5. Are there camping facilities available?
Yes, there are camping facilities available near the festival site. You can choose between regular camping or upgrade to VIP camping for a more comfortable experience.

6. Is Balaton Sound Festival only about music?
No, in addition to the incredible music lineup, the festival also features various art installations, water activities, beach parties, and other entertainment options to enhance the overall experience.

7. Can I buy tickets at the festival entrance?
It is advisable to purchase your tickets in advance as the festival is known to sell out quickly. However, if there are any tickets left, they may be available for purchase at the festival entrance, but availability cannot be guaranteed.

8. What should I bring with me to the festival?
Make sure to bring your festival ticket, valid identification, comfortable clothing and shoes, sunscreen, a reusable water bottle, and cash or card for purchases at the festival.
