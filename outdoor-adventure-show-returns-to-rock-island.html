Outdoor Adventure Show returns to Rock Island

March 29, 2024
blog

The QCCA Expo Center in Rock Island, is again hosting the Outdoor Adventure Show now through Sunday, Feb. 18, a news release says.

Hours for the show are Saturday, 9 a.m. to 8 p.m.; and Sunday, 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Admission is $8 for adults, $1 for children 6-15 and free for children under 6.

There will be a Kids Kamp area with a trout pond, minnow races and archery. Jim Crowley will be featured, plus new this year on Saturday is the Bass Summit at 3:30 p.m.

Mediator JJ Patton will lead discussions with anglers who have had success at the high school, college, local, state, regional and national levels.
