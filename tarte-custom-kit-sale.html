Usually, I hear the word “sale” and roll my eyes. It’s not very often that I click through to an advertisement for serious discounts and don’t end up disappointed. Usually, when a makeup brand has a sale they’re offering up a few trial size products at best, so when I heard about the Tarte custom kit sale 2022, which includes seven full-sized items, I quickly did a double take. You can literally rebuild your makeup case from the ground up for just $65 total—seriously, the kit even includes a beauty bag.

Here’s how it works: you choose seven products in different categories to build your bag. Pick something for your lips, cheeks, eyes and complexion, then grab a skincare goodie and a mascara. If you’ve been wanting to try some of Tarte‘s stand-out products like the creaseless concealer, the maneater mascara or a tartlette palette, now’s your chance.

Some of my favorite picks from the bag include the maneater mascara. I just layered this mascara on top of my already-on mascara, and it added serious length in just a single swipe. I’ve never actually seen my bottom eyelashes so big and fluffy in my entire life. Of course, you can use it alone as well for serious drama. The maracuja-tinted moisturizer is another fave. I’ve been struggling with flaky cakey-looking skin, and this barely-there tint is so hydrated with just the right amount of coverage.

There’s no shortage of things to choose from to build this kit, either—and did I mention it automatically includes a makeup bag? You can literally replace everything that’s low or not working for you in your current makeup bag and start over completely for less than $70. Given that everything in the bag is at least a $200 value, you’ll be saving so much money with this bundle. It’s a makeup miracle! But remember, the sale is live only for a short time, so if you’re looking to score on the deal, make sure to do it ASAP.

The Tarte website makes it super easy to navigate filling up your virtual makeup bag and choosing from the different categories. When you head over to the site, you’ll be met with a page that’ll take you through each step so that you fill up your bag and customize it to your liking. If you add everything up, each product is on sale for $9, event hose that are normally over $30. The Drink of H20 hydrating boost moisturizer that’s normally $39 is on sale for $9, and so is the Tartlette neutral eyeshadow palette, which normally goes for $21. No matter what you choose, the savings are definitely worth it.
