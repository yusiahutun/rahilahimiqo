Abbey Clancy is a British catwalk model and television presenter who is widely famous as the runner-up of Britain’s Next Top Model, Cycle 2, and the winner of the 11th series of Strictly Come Dancing in 2013.

Early Life of Clancy

Abbey Clancy was born as Abigail Marie Clancy on 10th January 1986, in Liverpool, England, the United Kingdom. Currently, she is 33 years old and her horoscope is Capricorn. Abbey is the eldest of four children of Karen Sullivan (mother) and Geoffrey Thomas Clancy (father).

Her mother, Karen was a member of the girl band group named Genie Queen. She grew up with her three siblings, two brothers Sean and John Clancy, and a sister Elle Clancy.

Her younger brother Sean is a footballer playing for AFC Telford United while her other brother John is a singer She establishes an interest in music from a very young age and formed an all-girl band called Genie Queen when she was just the age of 17 in the year 2003. Abbey holds British nationality and belongs to a white ethnical background.
