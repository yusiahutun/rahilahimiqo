Lupita Nyong'o Amazed by Her Dad's Emotional Story of Survival During Moi Era

September 06, 2024
blog
  • Black Panther actress was amazed at her father Professor Anyang' Nyong'o's past as an activist against an autocratic government in early 1980
  • The actress got to know that her uncle, her father's younger brother named Charles had mysteriously disappeared in what is said to be a ploy to intimidate her activist father
  • Lupita's father and his family later fled to Mexico City where the actress was born, the family later returned to Kenya

Oscar Awards winner actress Lupita Nyong'o has celebrated her father Professor Anyang' Nyong'o's bravery despite the injustices of the past regime.

Better future for Kenya

She said her father is passionate about Kenya and deeply believes there is a better future for the country.

According to Ancestry, a program that digs into a family's past, Lupita was shocked to learn that her father had actively taken part in pushing for democracy during former president Daniel Moi's 'iron fist' era.

The actress learnt that her father, who is Kisumu County Governor, was an activist during the reign of the then autocratic president.

Anyang' Nyong'o's brother mysteriously disappears

In 1980, Lupita's uncle, who is Prof Nyong'o's younger brother Charles, mysteriously went missing with reports indicating that he fell off a ferry.

The family doubted the events that led to their kin with whispers stating that he had been pushed off a ferry to silence Nyong'o who was a critic of the regime.

Out of fear Nyong'o and his family fled to Mexico City and while residing there Lupita was born in 1983.

She said:

"Well, for a long time we weren't really allowed to say his name. His name was always said in hushed tones and stuff. It was definitely a major loss to my family."

Better future for Kenya

In 1987 the Kisumu governor made a comeback to his homeland with his family in tow despite the risks associated with being a critic of the government.

Lupita stated of his father's return:

"My father is so passionate about the country and he so deeply believes that there is a better future and I know that he feels most useful when he is there."

Foot massage

On Tuesday, June 21, TUKO.co.ke reported Oscar Awards winner Lupita posted a lovely Father's Day message to her father Anyang' Nyong'o.

Lupita took to her Instagram page, posting a video of her adorably massaging her father's feet as they relaxed by the shores of a lake.

The actress conversed with her father as they had some enjoyable family times unwinding by the lake breeze.

She wrote:

"Happy Father's Day to the man whose feet I relish being at. #AnyangNyongo."
