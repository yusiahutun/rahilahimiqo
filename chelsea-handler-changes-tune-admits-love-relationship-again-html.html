Chelsea Handler, 44, admits she's had a change of heart and would 'love to find someone to love'

July 29, 2024
blog

She has been telling the world that she's perfectly fine living the single life, without a steady relationship.

But on Monday, Chelsea Handler, 44, revealed during an appearance on The Talk that she's changing her tune on the matter.

The chat show host confessed that her change of heart has come about after undergoing a period of therapy. 

Reversal: Chelsea Handler confessed therapy helped her realize that she would in fact like to be in a relationship during an appearance on The Talk on Monday

'I have changed my mind about being open to relationships, because I thought I was tough for a really long time,' the comedienne admitted.

'And what I learned through therapy is that being really tough is actually being vulnerable. And being able to admit that you want to be in a relationship, you know, and I thought I would sound weak if I said that.'
