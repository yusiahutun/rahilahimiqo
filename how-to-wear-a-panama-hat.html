How to wear a Panama Hat

July 23, 2024
blog

Christys' panama hats are hand-woven in Ecuador and blocked in its Oxfordshire factory to ensure the best quality.

"To find your perfect Christys' panama hat, try on shapes that best frame your face and proportions in a flattering way. For a short-brimmed hat, try our Carnaby Snap Brim, which offers you a classic style with a twist, or if a wider brim is preferred, try our Jessica style for a more chic summer look," says Noel Stewart, the creative director at Christys'.

Carnaby Snap Brim hat, £105, Christys'.
