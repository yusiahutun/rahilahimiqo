Best Heirloom Tomato Salad - How to Make Heirloom Tomato Salad

September 30, 2024
blog
mike garten

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 6

Total Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1/4 c.

    olive oil

  • 2 tbsp.

    Champagne vinegar

  • 1 tbsp.

    pure honey or agave

  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

  • 1 pt.

    assorted-colored cherry or grape tomatoes, halved

  • 2 tbsp.

    finely chopped fresh chives, plus more for serving

  • 1 lb.

    heirloom tomatoes, some sliced and some cut into wedges

  • Small basil leaves, for serving

Directions

  • 1. Whisk together oil, vinegar, and honey in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Add cherry tomatoes and chives; toss to combine. 

    2. Arrange heirloom tomatoes on a plate. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon cherry tomato mixture on top. Garnish with basil and additional chives.
