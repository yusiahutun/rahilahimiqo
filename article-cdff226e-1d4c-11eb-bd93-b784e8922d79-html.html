Laurel exec starts term as president of UMAA | Business

August 29, 2024
blog

OXFORD – The Ole Miss Alumni Association announced elected officers for 2020-21 at its annual meeting Saturday as part of the University of Mississippi’s Homecoming activities. This year’s meeting was conducted outside in front of the Triplett Alumni Center to provide more space and social distancing.

Lampkin Butts (BBA ’73), of Laurel, was selected president, a one-year term that changes each Homecoming. Butts is president and chief operating officer of Sanderson Farms Inc.
