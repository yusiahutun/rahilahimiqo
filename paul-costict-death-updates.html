Who was Paul Costict?

Paul Costict, member of the 90's rap collective B-Rock and The Bizz, has died suddenly at the age of 57.

What was B-Rock and The Bizz's first deal?

The quartet signed to Pound 4 Pound Records, a label based in Miami.

The signed after originally being discovered at a talent show.

B-Rock and The Bizz then released the local hit “Do Dat Dance” in 1992. 

Who was in B-Rock and The Bizz?

Members alongside Paul included Baron “B-Rock” Agee, his brother Leevirt Agee, Thaddeus “T-Bird” Maye and Costict.
