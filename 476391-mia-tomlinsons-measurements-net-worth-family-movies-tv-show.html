Acting

Mia has had a brief but rather noticeable career as an actress. In 2021, she was featured in the crime drama TV show; The Beast Must Die. In the same year, she also appeared in the Netflix docuseries, The Lost Pirate Kingdom, which is based on real events of 18th Century piracy in the Caribbean Sea.

In the series, she portrays Anne Bonny, an Irish pirate and one of the few women pirates during the Golden Age of Piracy. Besides these two major projects, she also appeared in the play The Funny Girls. The play is a fictionalized portrayal of 1950s rising stars Joan Rivers and Barbara Streisand before they became famous. Tomlinson portrays the character of Joan Rivers.
